best place in school

Cafeteria

The cafeteria is very big, with about 20 tables, each fitting about 20 people. To buy food there is a deli(sandwiches) line, a nachos and pizza line, and a daily special line. The daily special is a line with random food. Usually it has pasta or chicken. In a regular school year(no corona) there are four lunch times.

Gym

The gym is a very big basketball court. consisting of 6 basketball hoops. Each student has gym class(P.E.) twice a week. In gym class we have units based on different sports(basketball, soccer(football), ect.).

Hybrid and Remote schedules

Hybrid

Hybrid

Hybrid students have a two week schedule, they have a blue and a gold week. during a gold week students will go to school and be in classes. during a blue week students will be remote at their homes. blue and gold weeks alternate. there are 2 cohorts, cohort a and b. on the week of january 4th cohort a students will be in person and cohort b students will be remote.

Remote students

Remote students

Remote students, also known as RLA students, have a blue and gold week. during a gold week students will have classes such as math, history, english, science, and world launguage. during a off team week students will have classes such as music, drama, and tech apps. there are 2 cohorts, each designating when certain students have a blue or gold week. the week of january 4th will have cohort a students have a gold week and cohort b students have a blue week.
